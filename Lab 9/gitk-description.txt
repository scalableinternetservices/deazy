The upper leftmost window shows a history of commits in a hierarchical tree
structure showing the different branches, their relationships, the HEAD pointer,
and commit messages next to each commit.

The upper middle window show the author of each commit and their email address.

The upper rightmost window show the date of the commit in the format 'YYYY-MM-DD'
and the time in the military time format 'HH:MM'.

The section between the upper half and the lower half has the SHA1 ID, the row
that we are on, and a search box with various options.

The lower left window shows more information about the selected commit, such as
the author, committer, parent, child, branches, commit message, the changes in
diff format, etc.

The lower right window shows the files impacted by the selected commit.
